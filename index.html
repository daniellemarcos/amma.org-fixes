Toronto, Canada

Welcome to Amma's Toronto Satsang

Amma's Toronto Satsang group meets once a week, usually on Saturdays from 5-7 PM at Hilcrest United Church, 8958 Trafalgar Road, Georgetown, ON (see map on this page). During satsang, we chant the 108 names of Amma, have a short meditation, read and contemplate one of Amma's teachings, and sing bhajans (devotional songs). Satsang is followed by a vegetarian dinner at 7 PM. Everyone is welcome, including newcomers! For the most up-to-date information on our satsang schedule, please contact our Satsang Coordinator, Than Sookdeo. If you would like to find or give a ride to satsang, please contact our ride coordinator, Pranada Devi.
